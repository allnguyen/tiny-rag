Vector databases are specialized systems designed to store and search high-dimensional embedding vectors efficiently.

They support approximate nearest neighbor (ANN) search, allowing fast retrieval of similar vectors even in large datasets.

Examples include FAISS, Chroma, Pinecone, and Weaviate.

Vector databases typically store embeddings alongside metadata such as document IDs and source text. They optimize search using indexing techniques like HNSW or IVF.

They are a core infrastructure component in modern RAG systems because they enable scalable semantic search.
